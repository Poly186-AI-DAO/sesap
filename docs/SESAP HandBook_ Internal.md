# Executive Summary

This is a summarized version of SESAP’s internal handbook to help each and everyone understand the product and how we aim to present it to our prospective clients . It covers the overview of what SESAP is , the problem it solves , pricing model and the official roadmap

## Problem Statement for Poly186:

Poly186 is facing the challenge of building platforms, products, and services that provide the infrastructure for other companies to develop 3rd party platforms, products, and services that automate the production and distribution of basic needs. This process is complex and time-consuming, requiring the identification of the right partners, ensuring transparency and accountability, and managing funding and investment.

## Problem Statement for SESAP:

The outdated social contracts between individuals, corporations, organizations, and governments maintain systems of production and distribution that contribute to persistent problems such as climate change, poverty, food/water insecurity, social unrest, and other wicked problems. These contracts are unable to align incentives and coordinate the efforts of billions of individuals and organizations to solve these problems. This creates a self-destructive loop that perpetuates resistance to acting for the collective good. Despite advancements in technology and available capital, the lack of a platform to align incentives and hold individuals accountable remains a barrier to solving these problems. SESAP aims to address this problem by facilitating collaboration and coordination between organizations, individuals, and community groups, utilizing AI and smart contracts to align incentives, and implementing a reputation system for evaluating organizational credibility.

Poly186 is actively developing platforms, products, and services that provide the infrastructure for companies to build 3rd party platforms, products, and services that automate the production and distribution of basic necessities. To address the challenges of finding suitable partners, transparency, and accountability, and funding and investment, Poly186 has created the Self-Executing Social Agreements Platform (SESAP).

## SESAP Core

To align incentives and include a reputation system to evaluate the credibility of organizations. This enables Poly186 to effectively find and collaborate with the right partners, handle funding and investment transparency and accountability, and streamline the process of creating platforms and services to automate the production and distribution of basic needs.

## MVP ROADMAP

The SESAP MVP Roadmap outlines the incremental development plan for the self-executing social agreements platform. Starting with the simple UI for agreement creation, execution and monitoring, then gradually adding more advanced features like customizable contract templates, document management, and organization management, this roadmap covers all the key functionalities that you want to offer to the users. The final phase includes features like translation, recommendations AI, and discovery engine to make SESAP a complete platform for executing social agreements.

The MVP will allow customers to create, execute, and monitor agreements through a simple user interface, with added features and improvements along the way. By the end of the MVP phase, customers can expect a platform that allows for e-signatures, the ability to upload their driver's license for trustworthiness, the minting of contracts and agreements on the blockchain, customizable contract templates, and improved organization management through cloud talent and document management solutions.

SESAP MVP aims to provide customers with a simple UI for agreement creation, execution, and monitoring in Phase 1, with additional features such as e-signature functionality and document verification through the VERTEX VISION API.

### In Phase 2,

The platform will mint contracts and agreements on the blockchain, offer customizable contract templates, and analyze uploaded contracts using DOCUMENT AI.

### Phase 3

Will focus on creating company and organization structures, defining job postings, and improving the UI.

### Phase 4

Will bring cloud talent-powered features to handle organization management in SESAP, using both GPT-3 and Codex APIs to test applicants' understanding of organizations. Phase 5 will introduce the DocAI Warehouse for document management, intelligent products, and a discovery engine to increase conversion rates. Finally, in Phase 6, the platform will offer translation and language detection for agreements, custom recommendations, and a cross-industry discovery engine.
